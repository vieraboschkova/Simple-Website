Why did I create this simple website?

To learn of COURSE!

When I was 18 I had to make a choice, a choice no 18-year old person should be expected to take: decide what was I going to do with my future. I had two visions of it in my head. One path was leading to Faculty of Architecture, the other one to IT. Both at a technical university of course, as I always cosidered myself a mathematician with a special inclination for Humanities, Arts.


I chose Architecture.


Today, 18 years later, I choose a different path. I want to become a Front-End Developer. A perfect combination of the logical side of me with the architectural background that I have. Ahead of me, many years of studying, successes and failures.


A Brand New Future.
