Words, Words, Words

Passion for writing

Writing is another passion of mine. It started a long, long time ago, when I was a child. In Primary School I wrote songs. In High School, I wrote school-plays. At University I wrote poems and short novels. When I started working, writing became an after-hours activity.


I became a blogger. I wrote about my travels, cuisine, world news. I wrote about my adventures, what I've learnt and what surprised me.


I have a few ideas for novels, although they are a plan for the future. Retirement maybe. One day, someday.
